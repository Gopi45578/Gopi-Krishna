University of Nebraska at Omaha




University of Nebraska at Omaha UNO is located in the heart of Omaha, Nebraska, the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO) is a premier metropolitan university that combines the resources of a doctoral research institution with the opportunities the thriving community of Omaha provides. With global reach and vision, UNO is large enough to provide opportunities students seek, yet small enough to get the the Graduate Asistantship they need to achieve academic excellence, creativity, and engaged learning at competitive tuition rates.
